Threat Hunters Security

Responsible Disclosure

Last updated: June 18, 2026

Scope

Please report vulnerabilities affecting Threat Hunters application code, authentication flows, API routes, data exposure, or deployment configuration.

Rules of engagement

  • Do not access, modify, or delete another user's data.
  • Do not perform denial-of-service, spam, or social engineering attacks.
  • Share enough detail for reproduction and remediation.

How to report

Email a concise report with impact, steps to reproduce, screenshots or logs, and suggested remediation.
